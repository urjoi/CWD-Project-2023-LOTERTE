Hi, I'm Jhon Whency

I am a computer engineering student who is constantly fascinated by the ever-expanding world of technology and robotics. I consider myself an eager learner, particularly in the field of web development where I am just starting my journey with HTML and web technologies. In the realm of programming, I have ventured into languages such as Java, C#, C++, and Python, reaching an average proficiency level. I find immense satisfaction in solving problems and embracing the challenges these languages present, which fuels my desire to improve and learn more. With a humble understanding of SQL, I navigate the complexities of database management, aiming for efficiency and accuracy in every task. Additionally, my experiences with MATLAB and AutoCAD have allowed me to explore my creative side, especially in the context of engineering and design. Above all, my heart lies in robotics. The world of automation and artificial intelligence captivates me, and I am passionate about contributing to this transformative field. I find balance and inspiration in my side hobbies. When I'm not immersed in the digital realm, you'll find me on the badminton court, reveling in the thrill of the game and the camaraderie of my fellow players. Traveling, too, is a cherished pastime; each journey is a treasure trove of new experiences, cultures, and friendships waiting to be discovered. I am genuinely excited about the opportunity to learn and grow, both as a programmer and as a technology enthusiast. Join me on this humble journey as I explore the vast possibilities of the digital world and robotics, all while embracing the joy of continuous learning.
